Moodle 2.7 Filter for inserting dhtmlxCharts/Grids in moodle.
Replaces DIV's with class="eo_chart" into interactive editable
charts.
You can easyly construct scatter, line, spline, bar, pie and donut graphs and charts.
Requires moodle-atto_chart plugin.
Optionally install moodle-block_spreadman to manage your Chart and spreadsheet instances.
